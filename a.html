Understanding Soil

Why Soil Matters

Soil is a complex material made up of rocks and minerals, sand, clay, and soil organic matter, and hosts a diverse community of microorganisms. Soil reduces flooding, may help to mitigate climate change, and provides food for a growing world population. Changes to soil health affect us all. Read More

Threats to the Soil

Threats to soil health include urbanization, erosion, overfarming, and salination.

Land Use Change

Industrialized agriculture and production have changed the way that humans interact with the environment in important ways. Over time, these changes have had a big impact on soil health.
